Recipe for Shahi Paneer

Here is the list of Ingridients required:

  1. 200 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  2. 2 onions, finely chopped
  3. 2 tomatoes, pureed
  4. 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked in warm water for 15 minutes
  5. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1/2 cup fresh cream
  7. 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  8. 1 tablespoon oil
  9. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  10. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  11. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  12. 1 teaspoon garam masala
  13. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  14. Salt to taste
  15. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish


Steps to make Shahi Paneer are as follows:

  • Heat oil and ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  • Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  • Meanwhile, grind the soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste.
  • Add the cashew paste to the pan and mix well.
  • Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Pour in fresh cream and stir until well combined.
  • Add paneer cubes to the gravy and mix gently, ensuring the paneer is coated well.
  • Sprinkle garam masala over the paneer and mix gently.
  • Cover the pan and simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat, allowing the flavors to meld.
  • Once done, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve Shahi Paneer hot with naan, roti, or rice.
